BRANDS

Riverbank

2020

Personal Project

Riverbank was my first graphic design project, a nature-inspired apparel brand that combined my love for art and the natural world. Creating this brand solidified my passion for graphic design and set me on my career path. This was also my first dive into logo design, which has become another passion of mine.

C-Town Tees

2024

Personal Project

C-town Tees was a summer project creating hand-drawn, stylish t-shirts for different college towns. Every element—from the custom typography to the representational icons for each school—was drawn by hand. Though never fully completed, it was a fun exploration of translating campus identity into wearable art through illustration.
