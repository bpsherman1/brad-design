CAMPAIGNS

Stop Climate Change, Prevent Wildfires

2024

UW-Madison

This project was inspired by the increasing threat of wildfires and explores the link between climate change and its growing intensity. For this campaign, I created a series of visuals including posters, editorial layouts, advertisements, and mockups. I wanted to highlight how easily a wildfire could start with the match, but also the devastation it could create.

Protect our Parks

2024

UW-Madison

Protect our Parks was a project I created after an inspiring trip out west to Yellowstone and the Grand Tetons. I wanted to design fun posts to highlight the natural beauty while drawing attention to things affecting these places. There are three rows, each illustrated with a different style.

Cancer Won't Wait, Will You?

2024

Saatchi & Saatchi Wellness

Cancer Won't Wait, Will You? is my final intern project in 2024. It was a collective work with 4 other interns, but as the art director, I was responsible for all the poster and ad designs, as well as the overall campaign design and slides.
